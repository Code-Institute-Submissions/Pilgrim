If there's anything that is good for the soul, it's hiking. Over the course of Human history people have been walking everywhere, so much so, that walking in itself has become a sacred activity. Every year millions of people start walking, each one of them for different reasons. You might want a challenge or to go experience nature. Perhaps you want to hike for meditative or spiritual purposes. Whether you want any of that or just to experience culture and people in a non-orthodox way of travelling, Europe has great routes in which you can experience whatever you seek!

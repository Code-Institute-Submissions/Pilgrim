If there's anything that is good for the soul, it's hiking. Over the course of Human history people have been walking everywhere, so much so, that walking in itself has become a sacred activity. Every year millions of people start walking, each person for different reasons. You might want challenge or to go into nature. Perhaps you want to hike for meditative or spiritual purposes. Whether you want any of that or just to experience culture and people in a non-orthodox way of traveling, Europe has great routes in which you can experience whatever you seek!
